Description

Race-ready alloy frame with hydroformed tubing and solid dropouts

The Helium 20" 2020 Aluminum BMX Race Frame by Radio is the new and updated version of the popular flagship Helium frame. It's made from light and high-grade alloy, comes with hydroformed tubing and CNC'd dropouts. All combined, this frame provides a great strength-to-weight ratio and will surely help you to shove off some seconds on the track.

Technical features

  • Super light and hydroformed construction made from 6061-T6 aluminum
  • Incredibly stiff rear end thanks to its special tubing
  • Models with 20mm dropouts include chain tensioners
  • Disc-brake compatible
  • Forged and CNC'd dropouts for maximum strength
